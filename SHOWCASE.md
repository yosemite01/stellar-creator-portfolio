## 🎨 Design System

### Color Palette

#### Light Mode (Modern & Clean)
```
Background:     White (oklch 0.98) - Bright, clean workspace
Foreground:     Dark Gray (oklch 0.15) - High readability
Primary:        Deep Indigo (oklch 0.35) - Trust & professionalism
Accent:         Vibrant Teal (oklch 0.6) - CTAs & highlights
Secondary:      Soft Slate (oklch 0.5) - Supporting elements
Muted:          Light Gray (oklch 0.92) - Subtle backgrounds
```

#### Dark Mode (Sophisticated & Professional)
```
Background:     Deep Dark (oklch 0.12) - Reduces eye strain
Foreground:     Light Text (oklch 0.95) - Perfect contrast
Primary:        Bright Indigo (oklch 0.65) - Stands out beautifully
Accent:         Bright Teal (oklch 0.7) - Vivid highlights
Secondary:      Dark Slate (oklch 0.35) - Subtle depth
Muted:          Dark Gray (oklch 0.28) - Soft elements
```

### Typography
- **Font Family:** Geist (Modern, professional, highly legible)
- **Heading Size:** 32px - 56px
- **Body Size:** 16px (base)
- **Line Height:** 1.5 (relaxed, comfortable reading)

### Spacing System
- Base Unit: 4px
- Common Sizes: 4px, 8px, 12px, 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px, 64px
